What's the difference between dropping a class and withdrawing?
Generally, dropping a class early (before the drop deadline) removes it from your transcript entirely. Withdrawing later (before the withdrawal deadline) usually results in a 'W' grade on your transcript, which doesn't affect your GPA but indicates you left the course. Deadlines and specific policies vary greatly by university, so always check your official academic calendar and registrar's website!
