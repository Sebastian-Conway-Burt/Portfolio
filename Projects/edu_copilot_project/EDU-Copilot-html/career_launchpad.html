Cover Letter Basics

A cover letter introduces you and highlights your relevant qualifications for a specific job. Key tips:

  • Address it to a specific person if possible.
  • Tailor it to EACH job application – don't use a generic one.
  • Explain *why* you want to work for *that* company.
  • Connect your skills/experience directly to the job requirements.
  • Keep it concise (usually 3-4 paragraphs).
  • Proofread carefully!
